iBWIM: Demonstrations and Case Studies

Our test station at Tiffen, Austria

We maintain a test station on the Bridge at Tiffen where we can conduct long term tests of new equipment, and as a source of test data. Here you will find a description of the bridge and its current instrumentation (though this may change). Here you will find up to date statistic about traffic over the bridge.
