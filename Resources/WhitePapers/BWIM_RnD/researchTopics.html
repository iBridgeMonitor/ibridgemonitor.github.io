Our research topics in BWIM

iBWIM is one of our core products and we are active in the research and development of BWIM systems. Standard BWIM performs well on most bridges: our research is aimed at widening the range of bridges to which BWIM can be accurately applied. We conduct both self-funded proprietory research and work within EU and governmental funded research consortia. Our R&D effort covers the whole signal chain, from optimising our sensors and electronics to applying the pattern recognition techniques to improve estimation and to classify vehicles. However our currently active topics are:

  • applying signal processing techniques to improve the quality of the iBWIM signal.
  • developing advanced vehicle class identification systems, and
  • investigating optimisation for parameter estimation
